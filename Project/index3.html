E-COMMERCE WEBSITE BUILT WITH MERN STACK

Glaze & Grace is a creative e-commerce website developed as an individual placement project for the Edunet Foundation. It reflects my passion for clean design, simplicity, and natural aesthetics—values that are central to both the brand and the user experience. The site is fully responsive and dynamic, ensuring a seamless and engaging shopping experience across all devices, whether accessed from a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Led by an artist committed to elegance, mindfulness, and craftsmanship, Glaze & Grace features products with clean lines, pure forms, and a timeless style that speaks to those who value intentional living. Every item is crafted using high-quality, natural materials, celebrating imperfections that add unique character and warmth. The collection includes handmade ceramic pieces, elegant silver jewellery, and handpicked vintage finds— all curated to tell a visual story and evoke emotional connection. My design philosophy emphasizes slowing down, being intentional, and staying true to personal values. For me—and for Glaze & Grace—the guiding principle is simple: live better, not faster. This philosophy is woven into every element of the website, from the gentle color palette and clean UI to the thoughtful flow of navigation and user interaction. It's not just an e-commerce platform; it’s a digital reflection of mindful living and purposeful creativity.

Passion is energy, and this project is a true reflection of that energy. I believe that when you create with heart and genuine excitement, it naturally shines through the product. This enthusiasm drives the user experience and invites others who share similar values to engage. Glaze & Grace is designed for individuals who value thoughtful craftsmanship, minimalist beauty, and meaningful experiences. Nature has played a significant role in shaping my vision and ideas. I spend time outdoors daily—whether walking through a park, tending to a garden, or simply observing the sky. These moments of connection with nature slow me down and give space for inspiration to flow. Many design decisions, from earth-tone color schemes to organic textures, were influenced by natural elements like leaves, stones, and specific flowers I’ve encountered. These natural cues are subtly infused into the aesthetic of the website and product presentation. In building Glaze & Grace, I also aimed to push my technical skills by developing a complete, real-world application using the MERN stack— MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js. This allowed me to integrate a clean and modern front-end with a secure and scalable back-end. The result is a fully functional website that offers real-time updates, secure authentication, smooth user flow, and responsive design tailored for a beautiful experience on all screen sizes. This individual project not only showcases my full-stack development abilities but also reflects my values, creativity, and commitment to building something that is both technically sound and emotionally meaningful.
