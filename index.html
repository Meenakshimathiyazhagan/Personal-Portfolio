About me!

I am Meenakshi Madhiyazhagan. I recently completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Dr. MGR Janaki College of Arts and Science for Women. During my college years, I worked on several projects that helped me build strong skills in web development and AI. One of my main projects was an AI Chat Bot that used natural language processing (NLP) to talk with users. It could answer questions, help with tasks, and work smoothly across different devices. I also made sure it could keep learning to give better answers over time. Another project I created was Insight Stream, a news platform that shows news updates in a clear and simple way. It included real-time alerts, support for different languages, and tools like topic grouping and sentiment analysis to help users understand the news better. I also built an online shopping website called Glaze & Grace using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js). It had a clean design, live updates, and secure payments—making shopping easy and safe. This project showed my skills in building full websites from start to finish.
